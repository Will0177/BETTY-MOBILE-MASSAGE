About

Calm, professional, in-home care

Betty is a licensed, insured mobile massage therapist delivering restorative sessions to homes, offices, and private events. With a background in Swedish, deep tissue, prenatal, and sports recovery, every booking includes fresh linens, a portable table, music, and a grounding presence that makes your space feel like a spa.

Training

Certified massage therapist, continuing education in myofascial release and prenatal safety.

Approach

Slow, attentive pacing with frequent check-ins to match pressure and comfort.

Coverage

Mobile across metro area; travel beyond city core available by request.

Process

How a session flows

  1. Pre-visit consultation to learn your focus areas.
  2. Arrival with table, linens, bolsters, and music.
  3. Pressure calibration and breathing warm-up.
  4. Targeted bodywork with mindful pacing.
  5. Hydration reminder and simple stretch guidance.
